# PAYE (Pay As You Earn) and TIN Registration — Sri Lanka

Administered by the **Inland Revenue Department (IRD)**: **https://www.ird.gov.lk**

## Tax Identification Number (TIN)

Almost all tax matters (income tax, VAT, PAYE, etc.) require a **Tax Identification Number (TIN)**.

### How to obtain a TIN

1. Visit **IRD e-Services** or an IRD office.
2. Complete individual or business registration as applicable.
3. Provide NIC/passport, contact details, and nature of income/business.
4. Receive TIN confirmation — keep it for all future filings and employer declarations.

**e-Services portal:** https://www.ird.gov.lk/en/eservices/

## PAYE — Pay As You Earn

**PAYE** is withholding tax on **employment income**. Employers deduct tax from salaries and remit to IRD.

### Employer responsibilities

- Register with IRD as an employer where required.
- Deduct PAYE monthly from employees according to IRD tables / instructions.
- Remit deductions to IRD by the due date.
- Issue **annual PAYE certificates** to employees for their income tax return.

### Employee responsibilities

- Provide accurate details to employer (TIN, relief claims where applicable).
- Collect **PAYE certificate** at year end.
- File **income tax return** if required (e.g. other income besides salary, or to claim refunds).

## Income tax return filing (linked to PAYE)

Even with PAYE, you may need to file an **income tax return** if you have:

- Multiple employers during the year
- Business or investment income
- Foreign income remitted to Sri Lanka
- Claims for additional reliefs

**Filing page:** https://www.ird.gov.lk/en/eservices/sitepages/filing%20of%20return.aspx

## Corporate income tax (brief)

Companies pay **corporate income tax** under the Inland Revenue Act (rates per IRD Tax Chart for year of assessment 2025/2026 — e.g. standard corporate rates and concessions for SMEs may apply). Companies must file corporate returns separately from individual PAYE.

## Payment of taxes

- **Online** via IRD-approved payment channels (see e-Services).
- **Banks** — check current list of authorized banks on IRD website.
- Always quote **TIN** and tax type on payments.

# Sri Lanka Value Added Tax (VAT)

**Value Added Tax (VAT)** is administered by the **Inland Revenue Department (IRD)** under the **Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002** (as amended). Official portal: **https://www.ird.gov.lk**

## Standard VAT rate

| Period | Rate |
|--------|------|
| From **01 January 2024** | **18%** |
| Direct export of goods & certain zero-rated supplies (see VAT Act s.7) | **0%** |

(Previous rate was 15% until end of 2023 — confirm on IRD publications for historical periods.)

## Who must register for VAT

Registration is generally required when taxable supplies exceed thresholds (per IRD Tax Chart, with effect from **01 October 2024**), including:

- **Rs. 15 million** or more in a **one-month** or **three-month** taxable period, **or**
- **Rs. 60 million** in a **twelve-month** period,

or where IRD has reasonable grounds to believe thresholds will be exceeded.

**Note:** Any person carrying on a **taxable activity** may register voluntarily even below the threshold.

## SVAT (Simplified VAT)

**Simplified Value Added Tax (SVAT)** scheme may apply to eligible suppliers — see IRD:  
https://www.ird.gov.lk/en/Type%20of%20Taxes/SitePages/Simplified%20Value%20Added%20Tax%20(SVAT)%20Scheme.aspx

## Registration steps (overview)

1. Register for **TIN** if not already registered.
2. Apply for **VAT registration** through IRD (e-Services or prescribed forms).
3. Issue tax invoices as required; maintain purchase and sales records.
4. File **VAT returns** for each taxable period and pay VAT due.

## Filing & payment

- VAT returns are filed per assigned **taxable period** (monthly/quarterly as determined by IRD).
- Use **IRD e-Services** where available: https://www.ird.gov.lk/en/eservices/
- Keep VAT invoices, credit notes, and import/export documentation.

## VAT on financial services

VAT on financial services (value addition) is charged at **18%** with effect from **01 January 2022** (per IRD Tax Chart).

## Tourist VAT refund

Tourist VAT Refund Scheme (TVRS) may apply to qualifying tourists — see IRD TVRS page.

## Related taxes

Businesses may also encounter **SSCL** (Social Security Contribution Levy), **Stamp Duty**, etc. — see IRD “Type of Taxes” menu.

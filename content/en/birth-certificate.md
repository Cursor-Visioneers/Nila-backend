# Birth Certificate Application

## Required Documents
- Parent's NIC or Passport
- Hospital birth notification (BDR 1 form)
- If home birth: Midwife/GN confirmation letter

## Fees
- Standard: LKR 100
- Urgent (3 days): LKR 1,000

## Where to Apply
**Divisional Secretariat** of the birth registration area.
All 331 DS offices island-wide accept applications.

## Forms
- FORM: BDR-1 (Birth Registration Form) | https://www.registrar-general.gov.lk/forms
- OFFICE: Divisional Secretariat | https://www.dsboffice.gov.lk
- LAW: Births and Deaths Registration Act No. 17 of 1951

## Processing Time
- Normal: 7–14 working days
- Urgent: 3 working days
